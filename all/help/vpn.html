VPN

This applet offers a VPN connection to a virtual machine within Duke's network, allowing you access to Duke's resources for all devices connected to your Guardian Devil. This works slightly differently from DukeVPN, as your Guardian Devil connected to that specific virtual machines server.


Guardian Devil will also scan new devices when they connect, and you will receive a notification if they seem suspicious.


By toggling the status of your VPN, you will affect all devices connected to Guardian Devil, so any current processes may be briefly interrupted.


VPN directs your internet traffic to a Duke virtual machine, then forwarding it to its proper destination. This adds a layer of anonymity to your internet traffic, useful for secure transactions.


To set up your VPN connection to a virtual machine, see VPN Set-Up.
