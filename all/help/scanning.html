Scanning

This applet conducts automatic port scans of all devices on your network. You can change the frequency of these scans as you would like. By using this feature, you agree to have Guardian Devil use nmap to scan your network and the ports of connected devices. After scanning, Guardian Devil will generate a summary of all open ports and their services. While it is not uncommon for 1 or 2 ports to be open if a specific service is running, you should further investigate any ports that you have not opened manually.


Guardian Devil will also scan new devices when they connect, and you will receive a notification if they seem suspicious.
