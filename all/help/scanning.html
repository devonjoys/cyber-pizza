Scanning

This applet conducts automatic port scans of all devices on your network. You can change the frequency of these scans as you would like. By using this feature, you agree to have Guardian Devil use nmap to scan your network and the ports of connected devices. After scanning, Guardian Devil will generate a summary of all open ports and their services. While it is not uncommon for 1 or 2 ports to be open if a specific service is running, you should further investigate any ports that you have not opened manually. You can opt to receive an email notification if a suspicious port is detected.


Guardian Devil will also scan new devices when they connect, and you can opt to receive a notification if they seem suspicious. You can change these notifications on the Settings page.


On the scan page, you can run a manual scan of connected devices. This may take a few moments, especially if you have several devices connected. After the scan completes, you will be able to view a summary of the most common open ports on connected devices, as well as a summary for each device.
